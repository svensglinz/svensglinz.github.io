US Election Financing

An analysis of FEC Donations for Presidential Elections between 1992 and 2020 in R

POST_DATE

Research Question

Presidential Election Campaings in the United States are known to require substantial sums of money. By law (source), all donations above 200$ must be reported by every candidate together with information on the donor such as his/her address, the full name, the amount donated and ...

In this project, R is used to clean the data downloaded from []. A gender is then assigned to each name and finally, we use ggplot to create some interesting graphs about donation behavior across different election cycles, party affiliations and gender.
